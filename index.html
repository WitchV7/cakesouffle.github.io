How To Make a Souffle Cake


A soufflé cake is a type of dessert known for its delicacy, lightness, and airy texture. The term "soufflé" itself

comes the French language, meaning "to puff" or "to swell. "Soufflé cake is created by combining a basic batter

with beaten egg whites, providing the cake with a soft and fluffy texture.


Ingredients :

- 3 eggs, separated into whites and yolks

- 1/4 cup granulated sugar

- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

- 1 cup milk

- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

- Butter for greasing the dish


Steps :

1. Heat a pan or a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

2. egg whites and yolks. Place egg whites in a large bowl and egg yolks in a smaller bowl.

3. Beat egg whites with a mixer until foamy. Gradually add sugar and continue to beat until stiff peaks form.

4. In the smaller bowl, mix flour with egg yolks until well combined.

5. Heat milk in the pan until it's almost boiling. Then, pour a little hot milk into the egg yolk mixture while constantly stirring to prevent the yolks from curdling.

6. Once the egg yolk and milk mixture is well combined, pour it back into the pan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat.

7. Combine the egg yolk mixture with the beaten egg whites. Add vanilla extract and gently fold until well combined.

8. Grease a soufflé dish with butter.

9. Pour the soufflé mixture into the greased dish.

10. Heat the pan or skillet over low heat and place the soufflé dish on it. Cover the pan or skillet with a lid or aluminum foil.

11. Cook the soufflé for about 15-20 minutes or until it puffs up and sets.

12. Remove the soufflé from the pan or skillet and serve immediately
